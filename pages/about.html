About Us

A boutique law firm committed to delivering exceptional legal solutions

Nizar & Partners is a boutique law firm fully operating in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We pride ourselves on delivering top-notch traditional and non-traditional legal services tailored to clients across all financial capabilities.

Our firm stands out for our comprehensive approach to legal practice, combining extensive litigation experience with corporate and commercial expertise. We understand that legal challenges require not just knowledge of the law, but also strategic thinking and a deep commitment to our clients' success.

With a team of highly qualified advocates and solicitors, we have successfully represented clients in matters ranging from high-profile criminal defense cases to complex commercial transactions. Our lawyers bring both local expertise and international qualifications, including admissions to the Bar of England & Wales.
