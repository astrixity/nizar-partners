Practice Areas

Construction and Engineering

The firm provides specialized expertise in Construction and Engineering law, offering comprehensive legal support across the entire project lifecycle. This includes meticulously reviewing and drafting critical agreements (e.g., Turnkey, EPC, O&M) and providing strategic advice on rights, risks, and liabilities.

For disputes, we offer tailored solutions through Mediation, Adjudication (under CIPAA), Arbitration, or Litigation, matching the method to the dispute's severity and nature.

Civil and Criminal Litigation

The firm offers robust representation in all Civil and Criminal Litigation, handling complex disputes up to the appellate courts. In civil matters, we manage contract, corporate, commercial, and land issues with proactive case strategies.

For criminal defense, the team vigorously defends clients, ensuring constitutional rights are protected. We provide decisive, client-focused solutions through negotiation or aggressive trial advocacy.

Debt Recovery

The firm provides comprehensive Debt Recovery services for commercial and financial entities, aiming for efficient recovery rates. This covers the full legal process, from pre-action advisory and suits for claims, through to post-judgment enforcement (including bankruptcy/winding-up proceedings and writs of execution).

Strategies are tailored to the debtor's profile and the matter, ensuring the realization of outstanding liabilities while safeguarding legal compliance.

Bankruptcy and Insolvency

The firm provides specialized counsel in all aspects of Bankruptcy and Insolvency, representing creditors, debtors, and appointees (liquidators, receivers, judicial managers). The practice covers corporate and individual insolvency, focusing on restructuring, rehabilitation, and asset recovery.

This includes advising on creditor rights, managing winding-up and judicial management applications, and protecting client interests through negotiated workouts or assertive litigation.

Our expertise covers a wide range of regulatory matters requiring specialized legal knowledge and advocacy.
