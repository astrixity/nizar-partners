Profile

Farhan is an established practitioner who has significantly broadened his legal spectrum, handling and assisting in a wide variety of civil disputes. His practice encompasses commercial, corporate, insolvency, contractual, and debt recovery matters. He possesses a specialized focus on construction disputes, including statutory adjudication under the Construction Industry Payment and Adjudication Act (CIPAA) 2012. Adapting to modern regulatory demands, Farhan has also expanded his portfolio to include Occupational Safety and Health (OSHA) and Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) compliance, alongside employment and energy-related matters.

He is actively involved in dispute resolution, including arbitration, mediation, and other ADR mechanisms. Farhan has gained substantial experience advising a diverse clientele, including international banks, Malaysian and international corporations, statutory bodies, local authorities, and conglomerates across various industries.
