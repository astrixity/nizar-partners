Wan Azwan Aiman Bin Wan Fakhruddin

azwan@aaflegal.com

Professional History

Wan Azwan graduated from the University of Malaya in 2009. He began his career as a Deputy Public Prosecutor with the Attorney General's Chambers (2009-2014), conducting criminal prosecutions and appeals ranging from commercial fraud to capital punishment offences.

In 2017, he became one of the founding partners of Nizar & Partners. Throughout his career, he has appeared either as opponent to or alongside various prominent lawyers, including Tan Sri Shafee Abdullah, Datuk Hisyam Teh Poh Teik, and Sugumar Balakrishnan.

Expertise

  • Criminal defense (murder, drug trafficking, security offences)
  • Civil disputes and employment law
  • Contractual disputes
  • Court-assigned counsel in death penalty cases

Farhan Nizar Malek

farhan@aaflegal.com

Professional History

Farhan is an advocate and solicitor of the High Court of Malaya and a Barrister-at-Law in England & Wales. He read law at Northumbria University at Newcastle, United Kingdom, and was specially admitted to The Honourable Society of the Middle Temple in 2018, called to the Degree of the Utter Bar in 2021.

He joined Nizar & Partners as a Partner in 2023. Throughout his career, he has worked with international banks, Malaysian and international corporations, statutory bodies, and conglomerates in various industries.

Expertise

  • Commercial and corporate disputes
  • Administrative and insolvency law
  • Contractual disputes and debt recovery
  • Employment and energy-related matters
  • Arbitration, mediation, and dispute resolution
